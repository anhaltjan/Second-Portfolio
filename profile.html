How I design

A website is an investment for purpose, it doesn`t have to be beautiful, it just needs to be elegant, simple, focused furthermore reflective of the company.

  • Simple & Clean

    I dislike complicated, word and image heavy websites, using white space for emphasis is my preference.

  • Response & Load times

    keep the website fast and responsive to keep the interest.

  • funnel UI design

    funneling the views to where it matters: purchases, views, clicks, likes, comments.

  • reflective brand design

    whatever the item is, physical or digital; it needs to reflect the company's mission and public appearance.
